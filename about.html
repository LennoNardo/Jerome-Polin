Jerome Polin here!

I am known as a content creator and entrepreneur, who has always had a big dream to improve education in Indonesia.

A graduate of Applied Mathematics at Waseda University. A person who believes that all decisions in life are full of mathematical calculations. A person who believes that mathematics is not to be memorized, but to be explored.

My love for education, especially mathematics, led me to obtain a Bachelor of Engineering degree in Applied Mathematics at Waseda University through a scholarship program.
